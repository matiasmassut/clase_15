Stage of work

In my work, I follow the basic steps for productive collaboration and unique design

1. BRIEFING

We conduct an interview, and then i learn all the details of a project and the specific details of a business. I research competitors, collect references, form ideas for a project, and develop a structure for a future site.

2. PROTOTYING

Using the collected info I think over the structure of the website and the composition on each block, then I develope a description and schematic representation of the future project.

3. DESIGN CONCEPT

After approving the prototype by the client I start developing a design concept. On this step, I determine the most appropriate fonts, colors, and the site grid. Everything should reinforce each other.

4. FINALIZATION

On this stage, I create adaptative versiones of the websites and UI-Kits ready for transmission to the developers or assembling it on the constructor at your request.
